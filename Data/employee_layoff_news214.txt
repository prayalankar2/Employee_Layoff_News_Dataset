
  HBO is planning to lay off over 150 employees beginning as early as this week, Variety reports. 
  Citing anonymous sources, the report indicated that about 7 percent of HBO's 2,400 employees will be let go just weeks after other Time Warner units announced similar cuts. A representative declined to comment, according to Variety.
